Section3: How to sharpen a pencil with a knife
Learning Objective: Learn sharpening pencils step by step & Some tips

How to Sharpen Pencils

1. The first step is to place the blade flat against one of the edges of the pencils, holding the pencil with your non-dominant hand, use your dominant hand hold the blade. Propel the knife with your thumb of non-dominant hand towards the end of pencil.2. Always keep your motion to be slow. The wooden part can be sharpened a little thicker at first. When the inner core is nearly exposed, shave off thinner strips of wood slowly.3. Shave off a piece of wood at a time and rotate the pencil shaft constantly. As you do that, the wood is closing in on the graphite core and all sides of the pencil is even.4. Do not press the knife down on the lead core becuase this may break the graphite core and cause divots. After the exposed core reaches a desired length, you can carefully sharpen your pencil tip to a fine point with delicate hands.

Some Tips

1. Do not use a dull knife. You need more pressure to cut and it is easier to break the pencil tip.2. Keep the exposed lead sharp and long. However, it doesn't mean that the tip should be long as well. The exposed part should be like a long cone. If the sharp tip is too long, it is easy to snap.3. Slow down and do not rush. Protect the graphite core and yourself.
